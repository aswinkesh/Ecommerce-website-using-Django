Will big brands affect the native sellers on NativGoods?
No, big brands will not affect the sellers on NativGoods because our platform is exclusively dedicated to native sellers. We do not allow branded items on the platform, ensuring that small businesses and local artisans are prioritized and given a fair space to showcase their products without competition from large, established brands.
How can I start selling on NativeGoods?
Simply log in to your account and use the "Sell Product" or "Become a seller" feature to list your items by adding details like the product name, description, price,images, etc. Once submitted, your product will be visible to all users for purchase!
What are the seller fees?
Here are no seller fees on NativGoods. Our platform is completely free to use, allowing you to sell your products without any charges or commissions.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. All payments are secured and encrypted for your safety.
Why only small-scale sellers?
NativeGoods is committed to promoting local artisans, Home-makers and preserving traditional crafts. By focusing on small-scale sellers, we ensure authentic products and fair trade practices.
